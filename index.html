About the Conference - Secure

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

Research in fluid dynamics which is mostly Inter and multi-disciplinary has led to several innovations in the fields of aerospace, space, automotive, petroleum industry, medicine, neurosciences, drug delivery system, nanofluids and environment. Advancements in the above fields are quite rapid through the consistent efforts of researchers and academicians all over the world. In view of this, to create a platform for dissemination and discussions of the outcomes of the current research in the above fields, the Department of Mathematics, School of Engg & Technology in association with Research & Innovations, SNPSU, hosting an International Conference on "Advances in Fluid Dynamics" on 21st- 22nd March 2025. This Conference shall focus on: CFD, Multiphase flow, Combustion, Aerodynamics, Micro-fluids, Nano-fluids, Turbulence, Boundary layer, Energy Harvesting, Medical Device Design, Drug delivery system, Blood Flow, Transportation & Building System. This conference being inter & multi- disciplinary shall attract good number of participants from both within India & abroad who shall contribute Technical papers in the above mentioned focus areas. These technical papers shall be reviewed by the experts in the specific domains and the same shall be published as Conference Proceedings.
